The Layout

Parking is located in back of driveway, with lights illuminating to the front door where guests enter with their personal key code provided at booking.

After proceeding through the entrance, guests are welcomed into the shared spaces downstairs: a living room on the left and dining room on the right and a half bath down the hall. Private guestrooms and common office are located on the second floor: the Lavender Room includes an ensuite while the Rosemary and Sage Rooms share the hall bath.

The hosts Hope and Cobalt live downstairs and are happy to assist you throughout your stay as needed.

Activities

Walking distance to Starbucks & Rock City. 7 minute drive to Covenant College, Incline Railway and Ruby Falls. 10-15 minutes to Downtown Chattanooga. Near to Point Park, Guild Trail, Cravens House, Cloudland Canyon & more beautiful iconic sites!

Rock City Gardens

Experience this natural wonder featuring massive ancient rock formations, enchanting gardens, and the famous "See Seven States" panoramic view. A blend of natural splendor and whimsical charm makes this a must-visit attraction.

Ruby Falls

Descend 260 feet into Lookout Mountain to witness America's deepest commercial cave and largest underground waterfall. This stunning 145-foot underground waterfall is illuminated by colorful lights, creating a spectacular natural show.

Downtown Chattanooga

Experience the vibrant city life just 15 minutes away. Enjoy restaurants, shopping, and cultural attractions - including the Tennessee Aquarium and the Creative Discovery Museum - in this bustling downtown area

Lookout Mountain Incline Railway

Take a ride on America's most amazing mile, climbing the steepest passenger railway in the world. This historic trolley-style car climbs through the natural beauty of Lookout Mountain at a breathtaking 72.7% grade.

Point Park

Visit this historic Civil War site offering spectacular views of Chattanooga Valley and the Tennessee River. The park features military monuments, hiking trails, and interpretive exhibits about the "Battle Above the Clouds."

Covenant College

Former castle and luxury resort turned private Christian liberal arts college.
