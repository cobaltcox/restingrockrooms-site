Activities

Convenient access to many local activities on the mountain and off.

Rock City Gardens

Experience this natural wonder featuring massive ancient rock formations, enchanting gardens, and the famous "See Seven States" panoramic view. A blend of natural splendor and whimsical charm makes this a must-visit attraction.

Ruby Falls

Descend 260 feet into Lookout Mountain to witness America's deepest commercial cave and largest underground waterfall. This stunning 145-foot underground waterfall is illuminated by colorful lights, creating a spectacular natural show.

Downtown Chattanooga

Experience the vibrant city life just 15 minutes away. Enjoy restaurants, shopping, and cultural attractions in this bustling downtown area, including the Tennessee Aquarium and the Creative Discovery Museum.

Lookout Mountain Incline Railway

Take a ride on America's most amazing mile, climbing the steepest passenger railway in the world. This historic trolley-style car climbs through the natural beauty of Lookout Mountain at a breathtaking 72.7% grade.

Point Park

Visit this historic Civil War site offering spectacular views of Chattanooga Valley and the Tennessee River. The park features military monuments, hiking trails, and interpretive exhibits about the "Battle Above the Clouds."

Covenant College

Former castle and luxury resort turned private Christian liberal arts college.
