The Layout

After proceeding through the entrance, guests are welcomed into the shared spaces downstairs: a living room on the left and dining room on the right and a half bath down the hall. The hosts Hope and Cobalt live downstairs and are happy to assist you throughout your stay as needed.

Activities

Resting Rock Rooms is conveniently located atop scenic Lookout Mountain overlooking Chattanooga. Minutes away from uniquely stunning attractions such as Rock City, Ruby Falls and the Incline Railway. A great hub for outdoor adventure offering hiking trails, waterfalls, climbing, bouldering, hang gliding and water sports close by. Only 15 minutes from downtown Chattanooga, Southside, Riverfront and Northshore for all your urban amenities.

Rock City Gardens 2 min

Experience this natural wonder featuring massive ancient rock formations, enchanting gardens, and the famous "See Seven States" panoramic view. A blend of natural splendor and whimsical charm makes this a must-visit attraction.

Ruby Falls 7 min

Descend 260 feet into Lookout Mountain to witness America's deepest commercial cave and largest underground waterfall. This stunning 145-foot underground waterfall is illuminated by colorful lights, creating a spectacular natural show.

Downtown Chattanooga 15 min

Only a 15-minute drive to experience Chattanooga's large beautiful parks, vibrant arts scene, indulge in excellent restaurants, and enjoy premier shopping. You'll love the easy access to family-friendly entertainment, including the renowned Tennessee Aquarium, the Creative Discovery Museum, and the Chattanooga Zoo.

Lookout Mountain Incline Railway 7 min

Take a ride on America's most amazing mile, climbing the steepest passenger railway in the world. This historic trolley-style car climbs through the natural beauty of Lookout Mountain at a breathtaking 72.7% grade.

Point Park 8 min

Visit this historic Civil War site offering spectacular views of Chattanooga Valley and the Tennessee River. The park features military monuments, hiking trails, and interpretive exhibits about the "Battle Above the Clouds."

Covenant College 7 min

Former castle and luxury resort turned private Christian liberal arts college.
